Our Story

We saw how SMS was changing the way brands communicated with their customers, but the platforms were not built for long term success. Introducing Voyage Text - the best SMS messaging platform for eCom businesses.

We are a team built by finding solutions to the pain points we noticed working on our own DTC eCom brands. SMS has been the most powerful and successful ways of reaching customers, but we wanted to do it better.
